About Me

Malina "Mal" Morning is a web developer based in the Greater Seattle area. She enjoys collaborating with teammates to find creative solutions to complex problems. Whether part of a group or flying solo, she loves taking on new challenges. A natural problem solver, Mal's resourcefulness has become invaluable in her work. To the benefit of her projects, she keeps the bigger picture in mind when focusing on details.

Always motivated by a healthy curiosity, Mal enjoys learning about various facets of visual design, UX and UI, graphic animation and technical artistry. The creation of meaningful experiences using outside-the-box creativity is what drives her passion for tech.

Originally from the South, Mal found her true home in the Pacific Northwest. Her interests are many; and include travel, biology, astronomy, nutrition, gourmet cooking, cars, music, sci-fi, and gaming.
