About Us

Your New Window onto the Mediterranean

Our kitchen offers a variety of alternative Mediterranean foods straight from the sun-drenched shores of coastal cities. Our Eastern Mediterranean restaurant is renowned for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality, fresh ingredients. Nestled north of Seattle, you're just a stone's throw from a flavorful exploration of Greece, Cyprus, and Egypt.
